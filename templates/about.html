About This Simulator

What is this?

The Incident Response Simulator is an interactive training tool designed to help legal and policy professionals understand how to respond to common cybersecurity incidents. Through realistic scenario-based exercises, you practice making decisions under pressure and learn the legal, regulatory, and operational considerations involved.

Who is it for?

  • In-house legal counsel dealing with data privacy and security matters
  • Compliance officers and privacy professionals
  • Policy professionals working on cybersecurity regulation
  • Anyone new to incident response who wants a practical introduction

The IR Lifecycle

Scenarios in this simulator follow the standard Incident Response lifecycle:

1

Preparation

Policies, plans, and training in place before incidents occur.

2

Identification

Detecting and confirming that an incident has occurred.

3

Containment

Limiting the scope and impact of the incident.

4

Eradication

Removing the threat and root cause from the environment.

5

Recovery

Restoring systems and operations to normal.

6

Lessons Learned

Post-incident review to improve future response.

Scoring

Each decision is scored on a scale of 0–3:

  • 3 points — Best practice response
  • 2 points — Acceptable but not optimal
  • 1 point — Partially correct or incomplete
  • 0 points — Incorrect or harmful action

Aim for 80% or higher to demonstrate strong incident response competency.
